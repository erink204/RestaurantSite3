Who We Are:

The Codfather is the brainchild of Erin and Eddy Gossett. We are located at 333 Mulligan Ct.(behind Katy Lake) Waxahachie, TX. The original Codfather was opened in Ireland in 1970 by Eddy's father Jack (now sunning himself in Spain) so the duo decided to pay tribute to himr by opening the first and original food truck almost forty years on. The Codfather is a place for birthdays, catching up with friends, lunch in our courtyard in the sunshine, craft beer, cocktails and delicious food. We cater for many events including wedding receptions so enquire now by emailing erin.eddy.bookings@windmilltaverns.com
